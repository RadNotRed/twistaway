# Spotify brand assets

The Spotify icon and full-logo PNG files in this directory are the official
black and white RGB assets downloaded from Spotify's Design and Branding
Guidelines on July 15, 2026:

https://developer.spotify.com/documentation/design

They must remain unmodified and may only be used to attribute Spotify content
in accordance with Spotify's current Developer Terms, Developer Policy, and
Design and Branding Guidelines. They are not part of Twistaway's own brand.
